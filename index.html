Week 8: Data stories

This chart supports the statement made during the session “The next crisis?” in “Talking economics” conference by Dimitri Zenghelis. He mentioned that the cost of delaying taking climate action would be much more expensive than starting decarbonization now. Data from “The Cost for the Financial Sector if Firms Delay Climate Action” report by Climate Stress Testing and Scenarios Project is used. In the chart, as the point of time where we achieve carbon balance is delayed, expected loss from climate transition-related market and credit risk increases. This supports the statement that immediate decarbonization will lead to cost saving.
